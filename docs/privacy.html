Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2026

Pinterest Optimizer ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

When you link your Pinterest account, we collect your Pinterest username, profile image, and authentication tokens (Access and Refresh tokens). We also store the content you create within the app, such as Pin descriptions, images, and scheduling data.

2. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the app's core functionality: creating, optimizing, and scheduling Pins.
  • Connect to the Pinterest API to upload content on your behalf.
  • Analyze your niche preferences to improve AI-generated suggestions.

3. Data Storage

Your authentication tokens and app data are stored locally on your device in a secure database. We do not store your Pinterest password.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the official Pinterest API to facilitate account linking and Pin creation. Your interaction with Pinterest through our app is subject to Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
