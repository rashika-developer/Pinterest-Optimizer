📋 WHAT THE APP DOES
================================================================================

The app helps Pinterest creators automate content creation:

1️⃣ NICHE PROFILING
   User selects niche (Home Decor, Fashion, Food, etc.)
   ↓
   All suggestions filtered by niche

2️⃣ CAPTION GENERATION
   User describes image
   ↓
   App generates 3 captions:
   - Minimal & aesthetic
   - Engaging & conversational  
   - Sales-oriented
   ↓
   User picks favorite

3️⃣ HASHTAG GENERATION
   App generates 15 relevant hashtags
   Includes trending hashtags for current season
   Filtered by user's niche

4️⃣ PRODUCT FINDING
   Analyzes image content
   Finds matching products from:
   - Amazon (with affiliate links - you earn commission!)
   - Etsy
   - Other affiliate networks

5️⃣ AUTO-SCHEDULING
   Determines best posting time
   Schedules post automatically
   Posts without user action

6️⃣ ANALYTICS TRACKING
   Track post performance:
   - Views
   - Saves
   - Clicks to products
   - Engagement rate
   Optimize future posts based on data
